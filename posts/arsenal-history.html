The Story of Arsenal FC

Arsenal Football Club was founded in 1886 and has since become one of the most iconic clubs in football history. Based in North London, the Gunners have won numerous trophies, including 13 league titles and 14 FA Cups...

Recent milestones (2023–2024):

  • 2023: Signed Declan Rice, Gabriel Jesus, and Kai Havertz.
  • 2024: Finished as runners-up in the Premier League.
